Why Meditate with Us?

Simply Meditation Wellness Centre

Everyone is warmly welcome at Simply Meditation Wellness Centre, Dublin, and our meditation workshops that run throughout Ireland. Come and join us in our relaxed and friendly atmosphere. You do not have to have any previous experience with meditation to enjoy and benefit from our classes and events. We teach meditation and stress reduction techniques for daily life, to help everyone solve problems and find the peace and happiness we seek, in a way that is easy to understand and apply to everyday life. Simply Meditation Wellness Centre is run by Lynda Phelan and a friendly support team who are always happy to answer your questions and tell you more about our Wellness Centre. Drop in and experience the tranquility of the meditation room, browse our lovely little book shop and enjoy this peaceful oasis in the heart of Dublin City. We provide meditation classes, courses and study programs and everyone is welcome.

Why Meditate at All?

Simply Meditation Amazing Benefits

The purpose of meditation is to cultivate positive mental states and overcome negative habits. Through meditation we learn that happiness is a state of mind and that the real source of happiness lies within, not in external situations. When our mind is peaceful we are free from worries and mental discomfort, and we experience true happiness. Meditation is the method to make our mind peaceful and can be practiced by anyone.

Meditation is a vital way to purify and quiet the mind, thus rejuvenating the body
