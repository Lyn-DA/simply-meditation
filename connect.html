Directions

Simply Meditation Wellness Centre

We are located opposite Dunne & Crescenzi, in the same building as "The Doorway Gallery". Our front door is bright red. Upon arrival, please ring the bell and your therapist will release the door. We are located on the top floor of the building.

There is street parking out front, but it is very limited (make sure to put a ticket on your windscreen or use the parking app). There is also a Q-Park on Setanta Pl right around the corner from the centre. Alternatively, you can park around Merrion Square and walk down Nassau Street to get to us.

If you are using public transit, you may take the DART to Pearse Street or the LUAS to Dawson Street.
